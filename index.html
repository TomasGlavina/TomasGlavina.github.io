About me

Hi! I am Tomas Glavina. I'm 26 years old (90s kid!) and I'm from Buenos Aires, Argentina.
Currently, I am studying Software Engineering in Tampere University of Applied Sciences where I will be a tutor for the new students next year, which I'm very excited about!
I finished my first year with a good 4,69/5 overall grade and I already completed a 3rd year course 'Operative Systems and Linux Programming', because I love Linux and I wanted to learn more about it and programming C in the command line.

Besides school, I am also learning Full-Stack development with Django, Bash scripting and doing CTFs (usually Linux Forensics and Reverse Engineering). Also I am learning Data Structures and Algorithms, by reading Introduction to Algorithms by Thomas H. Cormen and applying them in Java.
My hobbies are going on hikes with my wife and dog, reading (The Hobbit, best book ever), playing football (Let's go Ilves!), learning more about coding and spending time with my friends.
